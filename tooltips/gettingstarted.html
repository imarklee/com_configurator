Getting started with Configurator & Morph

It looks like this is the first time you are running Configurator, so we thought you’d like a couple of pointers on using the system. In case you don’t already know, Configurator is our Template Management System (T.M.S.) and is used to control most aspects of Morph.

We have poured our hearts into both Morph & Configuratort hope you enjoy using it as much as we did making it!

Take a couple of minutes to go through the different help sections and once you feel ready to jump in, simply close this window and get started.

This help pane can be easily accessed at any time, either by clicking on the “Getting Started” button in the top shelf, or using on of the built in keyboard shortcuts (cmd + h).

Working in Fullscreen mode

We all know how having that extra bit of real estate can really help your productivity, so we decided to add a feature to Configurator to allow you to easily toggle between fullscreen mode and normal mode.
