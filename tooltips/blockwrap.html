Block Wrapping Explained:

The frontend of Morph is divided up into different blocks. Each "block" is a structural section (check the Help tab on the right to see a visual reference of these blocks) and has the option to enable an additional wrapping div around it.

We have done this to give you maximum flexibility when constructing your sites layout. Each blockâ€™s main div will be the width that you have defined in the General Settings of Configurator and will automatically be centered.

In the scenario where you would like to break outside of the fixed width layout, you can enable a wrapping block which will wrap around the outside of the block.
