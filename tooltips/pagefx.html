Understanding PageFX:

The concept of the inner & outer layouts require a bit of understanding to grasp, but once you do you will have unparalelled flexibility at your finger tips. Based on commonly accepted best practices, we refer to your sites main content as your primary content & likewise your two optional sidebars are referred to as your secondary & tertiary content. This naming convention relates to the importance of the content & is ordered accordingly in your sites source code.

Outer Sidebar

The outer layout refers to the width and position of your secondary content, whereas the inner layout refers to the width and position of your tertiary content.
